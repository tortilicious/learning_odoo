This module allows to force early payment discounts past the validity
date of the discount set through the payment term.

**Table of contents**

.. contents::
   :local:

Usage
=====

The early payment discount will be applied automatically, even past its
validity date, using register payment wizard if the "Force financial
discount" is used on the invoice.
